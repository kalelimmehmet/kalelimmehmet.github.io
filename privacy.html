Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 18, 2026

ARISTOKRATI INC. ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and disclosed when you use our mobile software applications.

1. Information We Collect

We limit the collection of personal data. Depending on the specific application you use, we may collect information you provide directly to us if you create an account, such as your name and email address. For applications that function fully offline, we do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data to our servers. We may also collect general, anonymized usage data and diagnostic information (such as crash logs) to improve our services.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to provide, maintain, and improve our app's functionality, process transactions, track user progression, and for service-related communications.

3. Third-Party Services and Advertising

Some of our applications may use third-party advertising networks, specifically Google AdMob, to display ads. These third-party services use mobile advertising identifiers (such as Apple’s IDFA or Google’s Advertising ID) to collect device identifiers, IP addresses, and general usage data to serve personalized advertising based on your interests. We do not sell your personal account information to these third parties. For more information, please review Google’s Privacy Policy and partner sites policy: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites.

4. Your Choices Regarding Ads and Tracking

Depending on your device, you can control how your data is used for personalized advertising. On iOS devices, our apps that display advertisements will request your permission to track your activity to deliver personalized ads (App Tracking Transparency). If you select "Ask App Not to Track", you will still see ads, but they will not be tailored to your interests.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

If your data is stored on our servers, we retain your personal information only for as long as your account is active. You have the right to request the deletion of your account and associated personal data at any time by contacting us using the information below.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, or wish to request the deletion of your data, please contact us at:
info@aristokrati.com
